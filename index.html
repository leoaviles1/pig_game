Pig Dice Game

New to the game?

Game Rules:

The game has two players, playing in rounds

Rule #1: A player rolls the two dice as many times as he/she whishes. Each result get added to their current ROUND score.

Rule #2: If the player rolls a 1, all their ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.

Rule #3: The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that their ROUND score gets added to their GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn.

Rule #4: A player looses their ENTIRE score when he rolls two 6's in a row. After that, it's the next player's turn.


Objective:

The first player to reach the winning score on their GLOBAL score wins the game!


To Start:

Input a winning score between 20-200 in the text box and click NEW GAME

If playing another round, the winning player will go first!
